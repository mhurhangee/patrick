# Getting Started

PatrickOS is a local-first patent drafting assistant. It works offline, runs on your infrastructure, and uses AI to help attorneys draft and respond to patent documents.

## Choose your deployment

There are three ways to run PatrickOS:

- **Local** — desktop app, everything on your machine, Ollama for AI
- **Self-hosted** — your firm runs the server and database
- **Cloud** — hosted by PatrickOS, your firm's data stays isolated

See the [Deployment](/docs/deployment) guide for setup instructions for each mode.

## The workspace

The workspace is where you do your work. It has three main areas:

- **Sidebar** — your documents, organised by type (disclosures, office actions, drafts, etc.)
- **Document viewer** — open and edit documents, split view for comparing
- **Assistant** — AI chat with context from your open documents

## Working with documents

Open a document from the sidebar to view it. The assistant automatically has context from all open documents — you can see what's in context at the bottom of the chat panel.

To add a new draft, click **New draft** at the bottom of the sidebar. To upload a PDF, click **Upload file**.

## The assistant

The assistant understands patent law context. You can ask it to:

- Draft responses to office actions
- Amend claims
- Summarise prior art
- Compare documents

It uses the documents you have open as context. You can remove a document from context by clicking the × next to its name in the chat input area.
