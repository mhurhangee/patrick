A **task** is a folder you already have, opened in Patrick. Your source documents
are never modified — Patrick reads them and proposes edits in separate, editable
copies you own.

## The brief

Each task has a short **brief** — a one-paragraph statement of the objective. It
frames everything Patrick does. Right after opening a folder, ask Patrick to
**draft the brief from the documents**.

## Notes

A running **notes** record (decisions, findings, strategy) lives in the sidebar —
you and Patrick both add to it as the matter progresses.

## Your files stay yours

Patrick never renames, moves, or deletes your originals. Drafts it creates are
clearly marked and live alongside them in the same folder, as plain `.docx`.
