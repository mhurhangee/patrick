Patrick is an **agent-first patent-prosecution assistant**. It drafts and redlines
office actions and claim amendments directly in your own Word files — as native
tracked changes you approve — and runs entirely on your own computer.

## Open · Transparent · Yours

- **Open** — Apache-2.0, and your work lives as plain `.docx` / `.pdf` in your own
  folders. No proprietary database, no lock-in.
- **Transparent** — you see exactly what the agent is told and doing: the system
  prompt, its reasoning, every tool call, and the running cost.
- **Yours** — everything stays on your machine, including your AI keys. There is
  no Patrick server.

## How it works

You point Patrick at a folder you already have (a matter), and it reads the
documents inside. Ask it to draft a response or amend a claim, and it proposes
**tracked changes** in a real Word file — accept or reject each one, just like a
colleague's redlines.
